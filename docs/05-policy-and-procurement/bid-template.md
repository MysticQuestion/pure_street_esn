# ESN Bid Template

This template outlines the key sections to include when responding to Requests for Proposals (RFPs) or bidding on municipal contracts involving environmental sanitation and civic technology.

## Executive Summary

Provide a concise overview of Pure Street, ESN, and the proposed solution tailored to the city’s specific needs.

## Problem Statement

Summarize the challenges faced by the municipality (e.g. lack of verification, fragmented data, reactive dispatching) and how ESN addresses them.

## Proposed Solution

Describe the ESN architecture, services, and how the platform will be deployed and customized for the municipality.  Highlight integration points with existing systems (311, GIS) and expected outcomes.

## Implementation Plan

Outline the phases (e.g. pilot corridor, city‑wide rollout), timelines, training, and resource requirements.  Include risk mitigation strategies and success criteria.

## Budget

Provide a detailed budget with costs for software development, deployment, support, training, and any hardware or licensing fees.  Offer alternative pricing models (e.g. subscription vs. fixed cost) if applicable.

## Compliance and Certifications

Address accessibility, privacy, data retention, and security requirements.  Include any relevant certifications or attestations.

## References and Case Studies

Include testimonials, previous pilot results, or references from other BIDs or municipalities that have used Pure Street services.
