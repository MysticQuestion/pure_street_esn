# Glossary

This glossary defines key terms used throughout the ESN documentation.

- **Corridor:** A contiguous block or sequence of blocks (e.g. a stretch of street) used as the unit of analysis for sanitation scoring.
- **Hazard Report:** A structured record of an observed environmental issue (illegal dumping, biohazard, infrastructure decay, etc.) that includes a photo, location, timestamp, category, and severity.
- **Verification Engine:** The service that validates reports by checking metadata, deduplicating near‑duplicates, and assigning confidence scores.
- **SLA:** Service Level Agreement—the target time within which a hazard must be resolved after verification.
- **BID:** Business Improvement District—an area within a city where local businesses pay additional fees for services like cleaning and security.
