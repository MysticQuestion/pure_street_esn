## Audit Brief Template

**Purpose:** To provide an independent audit committee or oversight body with a succinct overview of ESN’s data integrity, compliance, and performance.  This brief is intended for periodic reviews (e.g., quarterly or annually).

### Template Outline

1. **Scope of Audit**
   - Time period covered and data sources reviewed.
   - List of services audited (reporting engine, verification workflows, data storage, routing, notifications).
2. **Data Integrity Findings**
   - Verification error rates (false positives/negatives) and duplicate detection accuracy.
   - Compliance with data schemas and taxonomy definitions.
   - Sample of audit logs with timestamped actions and outcomes.
3. **Security and Privacy Compliance**
   - Review of access controls and role permissions.
   - Assessment of PII minimization practices and redaction procedures.
   - Summary of any incidents (breaches, unauthorized access) and remedial actions.
4. **Service Level Performance**
   - SLA compliance statistics (e.g., percentage of hazards addressed within target resolution times).
   - Downtime or outage incidents and how they were handled.
5. **Recommendations**
   - Areas for improvement in processes, documentation, or technology.
   - Suggested policy updates or additional training for staff and partners.
6. **Management Response**
   - Responses from Pure Street leadership or relevant departments to the audit findings and recommendations.
   - Action plan with timelines and responsible parties.

Audits are essential for maintaining transparency and trust.  Store finalized audit briefs in a secure, access‑controlled location and make public summaries available when appropriate.
