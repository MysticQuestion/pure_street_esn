## Grant Impact Report Template

**Purpose:** To demonstrate the measurable outcomes of ESN-funded projects to grantors and philanthropic partners.  This report focuses on the alignment of activities with grant objectives and the quantifiable impact achieved.

### Template Outline

1. **Project Overview**
   - Grant name, funding amount, and duration.
   - Objectives and activities supported (e.g., deploying ESN-Lite in three corridors, hiring local field crews, developing analytics).
2. **Key Metrics**
   - Number of reports submitted and verified.
   - Reduction in hazard density and response times compared to baseline.
   - Improvement in corridor scores across pilot areas.
   - Community engagement metrics (number of residents trained, outreach events held).
3. **Environmental Justice Outcomes**
   - Analysis of service improvements in EJ neighborhoods.
   - Comparison of outcomes for priority communities vs. citywide averages.
4. **Narrative Highlights**
   - Case studies or testimonials from residents and crew members.
   - Images (before/after) or maps illustrating significant improvements.
5. **Budget Utilization**
   - Summary of how grant funds were spent (personnel, equipment, outreach, technology).
   - Explanation of any deviations from the original budget and lessons learned.
6. **Sustainability Plan**
   - How ESN will maintain and expand program outcomes beyond the grant period.
   - Potential revenue streams (data licensing, municipal contracts) and new funding opportunities.
7. **Appendices**
   - Methodology details, supporting data tables, and relevant external references.
